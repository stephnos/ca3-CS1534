SF's FOG

San Francisco is known for its fog, the locals have even named it Karl. It has it's own Instagram account with 250,000 followers. Fog horns have been deployed throughout SF to warn ships not to hit anything in the blinding fog. Karl is more than a weather phenomenon, its the character and charm of the city. 

SF is built on more than 50 hills!

For this reason, the city is very popular with skateboarders. Not only this but SF is home to "Lombard Street" which is the most crooked street in the world, with windy hilly bends!
